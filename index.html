Hello all! My name is Sydney Wong, and I am a second-year computer science major at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD). I am an aspiring software engineer, a tutor for the UCSD Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) Department, and an advocate for women and minorities in computing!

Experiences

Bon App Boutique

During the Summer 2020, I was a Mobile Developer & Assistant Intern at the mobile consulting start-up Bon App Boutique. Although this internship was virtual, interning was a great experience! I learned a lot about Swift, Git, and Agile Methodology.

Women in Computing (WIC)

I presently serve on the WIC Board as the Outreach Co-Chair. My duties include co-leading Empowerment and Development for Girls in Engineering (EDGE), a mentorship program where local San Diegan high schoolers are paired with a UCSD engineering undergraduate mentor.

UCSD CSE Tutor

I am currently tutoring for my third quarter in a row! As a tutor, I assist professors with their intorductory Java and Python courses. Tutoring is an amazing experience because I get to work with so many hardworking and intelligent tutors and TAs. Every quarter, I learn a lot from the people I work with - especially the professors who deeply care about the welfare of their students. Additionally, I love helping and showing students the wonders of computer science!

I have tutored for the following courses: CSE 8A and CSE 8B.

Tutoring Responsibilities Some of my duties include conducting personal tutor hours, answering student questions, and grading.
